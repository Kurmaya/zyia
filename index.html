Hi!

I'm Lauren.

I'm passionate about living the best life and going after your dreams!

I am a sister, daughter, friend, leader, lighting designer, grad student, hiker, traveler... the list can go on and on.

I never really wore activewear until about 3 and a half years ago! I know crazy! I just never felt comfortable in what was in store (or it just didn't fit right). I was pushing 200 pounds and working 16+ hours behind a computer screen. But then I found something that made me feel not only comfortable but confident as well. It made a HUGE impact on the way I felt and thought about myself.

With the fast pace life that we've become accustomed to, it can be difficult to prioritize YOU! Everything you need gets put to the back of your mind while you focus on what everyone needs and/or wants from you. Trust me, I get it! Transitioning from a sedentary life (thanks to Covid) and a 9-5 job to the fast paced grind that is grad school in NYC, its hard to prioritize myself.

I created this group to build a community of people from all walks of life who feel the way I do. I wanted a safe and supportive place where people can come together, lift each other up, encourage each others' dreams, and have fun!

I promise to bring a huge amount of value to this group! As a grad student in NYC, I will also be sharing how I use a line of athleisure wear to stay active, confident, and comfortable every day.

I'm loving the community we're building here! A community dedicated to loving ourselves AND each other through whatever life decides to throw our way! I look forward to welcoming you into this community!
